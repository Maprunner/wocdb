About

The Maprunner WOC/JWOC database includes results from all WOCs (except B Finals) and JWOCs (except Qualification races, and excluding second relay teams). Select a menu option to try out the various queries available and see what you find. The information in the database has come from a number of sources, but most notably the records maintained by Bryan Teahan, Blair Trewin, the IOF, event websites, and various copies of CompassSport, Orienteering World and other things I happen to have around the house.
